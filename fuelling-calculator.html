Race Fuelling Planner

Set up your race profile with speeds and distances, add your food items to a timeline, and see real-time glucose and fructose absorption rates to optimise your fuelling strategy.

Absorption model

Glucose is absorbed via SGLT1 transporters at a maximum rate of ~60 g/hr. Fructose uses the GLUT5 transporter at ~30 g/hr. Combined 2:1 glucose:fructose products can deliver up to ~90 g/hr of total carbohydrate.

The graph models each food item's absorption over a window based on form: Liquid ~15 min Gel ~20 min Solid ~35 min. Absorption is modelled as a bell-shaped curve peaking at the midpoint of the window, capped at physiological transport limits (60 g/hr glucose, 30 g/hr fructose).

Individual absorption rates vary with training, gut conditioning, and race intensity. Always practise your fuelling strategy in training before race day.
