Methodology

Carbohydrate targets are based on current sports science consensus (Jeukendrup, 2014; Thomas et al., 2016):

<1 hr Mouth rinse only; minimal intake needed
1–2 hr 30–60 g/hr from single-source carbohydrate
2–3 hr 60–80 g/hr; benefit from glucose + fructose blends
>3 hr 80–120 g/hr using multiple transportable carbohydrates

Intensity multiplier adjusts targets: Low ×0.8 Moderate ×1.0 High ×1.2
Fluid baseline is 500 ml/hr adjusted by intensity: Low ×0.7 Moderate ×1.0 High ×1.3

Individual requirements vary. Always practise your fuelling strategy in training before race day.
