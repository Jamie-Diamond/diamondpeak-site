What is maji?

maji combines the analytical power of a top-tier consulting team with the accumulated expertise of over 100 projects across diverse manufacturing environments. We've distilled real-world problem-solving patterns, proven methodologies, and factory-floor insights into an AI platform that integrates directly with your MES systems. This means you get expert-level analysis of your actual operational data, informed by patterns and solutions validated across hundreds of projects available instantly, 24/7.

  • The Problem
    Many manufacturers find it difficult to keep improvement moving at pace once external support ends. Valuable knowledge often sits with individuals or in scattered documents, making it hard to maintain momentum.

  • ‍
    Why it Happens
    ‍    Traditional consultancy support often focuses on frameworks and presentations rather than building day-to-day operational capability. At the same time, teams may lack the tools, visibility, or structured methods needed to confidently identify and act on opportunities.

  • The Resulting Gap
    ‍    Without clear insight and repeatable ways of working, it becomes harder to measure opportunity, prioritise effectively, and build skills that last. This can slow progress and leave factories operating below their full potential.
