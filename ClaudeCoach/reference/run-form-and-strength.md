# Run form, drills, and strength programme

**TL;DR:** Diagnose form via Ochy (slow-motion video → form score, style class, weak points). Stryd quantifies the cost in running economy if available. Drill prescription is by *fault* (overstride / low cadence / vertical bounce / arm crossing). Gym programme is two sessions a week (lower-body power + stability), reduced in build/specific phases. Ankle-rehab priorities flagged inline.

> Closes the –20 min on the IM run leg via running economy gain + injury resilience. Pair with `run-execution.md` for race-day discipline and `templates/session-library.md` for session-design context.

## Diagnostic — Ochy + (optional) Stryd

### Ochy

Slow-motion video analysis app. Free basic analysis, no account required.

| Step | Detail |
|---|---|
| 1 | Download Ochy app. |
| 2 | Film yourself at easy pace — 10–15 sec, side-on, good light. |
| 3 | Upload, run analysis. |
| 4 | Get score, style classification, weak points. |
| 5 | Share report with Claude → form prescription via the **Form + strength** prompt in `prompts.md`. |

Cadence: every 6–8 weeks during a build to measure improvement.

### Ochy + Stryd cross-reference

Pair value: Ochy says *what* the issue is, Stryd says *how much it costs you*. Stryd not currently in kit — see `templates/session-library.md` Stryd section.

| Form issue | Ochy flags | Stryd confirms | The fix |
|---|---|---|---|
| Overstride | Overstride angle >5° | High GCT (>280 ms) | Higher cadence → shorter stride → foot lands under hips |
| Low cadence | "Cheetah" / low spm style | GCT elevated, form power % high | Garmin cadence alert, cadence drills, A-skip |
| High form-power % | — | Form power >28% | Cadence + posture + fatigue management |
| Vertical bounce | High oscillation | VO >7 cm | Forward lean from ankles, "run tall not bouncy" |
| Arm crossing | Arms crossing midline | Wasted rotational energy | Arm-swing drills, keep elbows at 90° |

## Drill prescriptions by fault

### Overstride / high GCT

Goal: foot lands under hips, not in front. Reduces braking force.

| Drill | How | Sets/reps | When |
|---|---|---|---|
| A-skip | Skip with high knee drive, foot strikes directly under hip. "Paw back" not "reach forward". | 3 × 20 m | Before easy runs |
| Cadence strides | 20 sec at target cadence (174–178 spm), focus quick light footstrike | 4–6 × 20 sec | Before quality sessions |
| Metronome runs | Garmin cadence alert at target spm. Run 10 min matching beep. | 1–2 × 10 min | Weekly easy run |
| Wall drill | Stand close to wall, drive knee up until shin parallel, hold 2 sec, lower under control | 3 × 10 each | Any time |

### Low cadence / "Cheetah" style

Goal: increase stride rate without shortening stride excessively. More turnover, same power.

| Drill | How | Sets/reps | When |
|---|---|---|---|
| High knees | Fast mini-steps, knees hip height, arms pumping | 3 × 20 m | Pre-run activation |
| Fast feet | Very short, very fast on the spot, 10 sec | 5 × 10 sec | Pre-run |
| Cadence build | Start easy run at current cadence, increase by 5 spm every 5 min | 1 × 20 min | Weekly |
| Downhill strides | Gentle decline, gravity-assisted faster turnover, 80 m controlled | 4–6 × 80 m | Post easy run |

### High vertical oscillation

Goal: more horizontal propulsion, less vertical bounce.

| Drill | How | Sets/reps | When |
|---|---|---|---|
| Low-ceiling run | Imagine running under a low ceiling. Slight forward lean from ankles. | 3 × 2 min | During easy runs |
| Bounding | Long, low bounds — horizontal distance not height | 3 × 30 m | Post easy run |
| Posture check | Every 5 km on long runs: chin up, chest tall, lean from ankles not waist | — | Race + long-run habit |

## Strength programme

Runner-specific. Everything here improves running economy, injury resilience, or power output directly.

### When to lift

| Phase | Frequency | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| Base | 2–3×/wk | After easy runs or evenings |
| Build | 2×/wk | After easy runs — never before key sessions |
| Specific | 1–2×/wk | Reduce load |
| Taper | 1×/wk or none | First half of taper only |

### Session A — Lower-body power (Tuesday-style)

| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Bulgarian split squat | 3 × 8 each | Single-leg power — mirrors running stride |
| Romanian deadlift | 3 × 8 | Hamstring strength — most undertrained runner muscle |
| Step-ups (high box) | 3 × 10 each | Quad + glute activation |
| Single-leg calf raise | 3 × 15 each | Achilles resilience — prevents #1 runner injury |
| Hip thrust | 3 × 12 | Glute max power — primary propulsion muscle |

### Session B — Stability + injury prevention (Thursday-style)

| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Single-leg deadlift | 3 × 8 each | Balance, hamstring, glute |
| Nordic hamstring curl | 3 × 6 | Best injury-prevention exercise for runners |
| Lateral band walk | 3 × 20 steps | Glute medius — prevents IT band and knee issues |
| Dead bug | 3 × 10 each | Deep core — prevents late-race forward collapse |
| Copenhagen plank | 3 × 20 sec each | Inner-groin and hip stability |

### Injury-prevention priority order (general)

| Priority | Exercise | Prevents |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Single-leg calf raise | Achilles tendinopathy |
| 2 | Nordic hamstring curl | Hamstring strains |
| 3 | Lateral band walk | IT band, runner's knee |
| 4 | Bulgarian split squat | General imbalances |
| 5 | Dead bug | Lower-back, pelvic instability |

### Ankle-rehab priority (CURRENT, until cleared)

> **Override the general priority list while ankle is in rehab.** These four take precedence for peroneal / ankle resilience:
>
> 1. Single-leg calf raise — graded loading, 3 × 15 each side daily during ramp-back.
> 2. Single-leg balance work (eyes-closed, foam pad progression) — proprioception.
> 3. Copenhagen plank — hip stability supports landing mechanics.
> 4. Lateral band walk — glute medius, indirectly protects ankle from valgus collapse.
>
> Pain-led, not plan-led. Increase load only if pain ≤2/10 during and 0/10 next morning.

## Daily mobility (10 min)

| Exercise | Duration | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Hip-flexor stretch (couch stretch) | 60 sec each | Sitting shortens hip flexors |
| Calf + Achilles stretch | 45 sec each | Prevents #1 runner injury |
| Pigeon pose | 60 sec each | Hip rotators |
| Thoracic rotation | 10 reps each | Arm-swing efficiency |
