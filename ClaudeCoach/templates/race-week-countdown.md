# Race-week countdown — IM Italy Emilia-Romagna 2026

**Race:** Saturday 19 September 2026, Cervia. **Day-by-day from 7 days out.** Use this as the structured fill-in for the **Pre-race week countdown** prompt.

> Heat-acclimation maintenance through race week is non-negotiable — adaptation decays within 7–10 days. 3–4 hot baths in the final 7 days.
> Athlete declines special-needs bags. T1/T2 ice strategy is athlete-supplied via insulated soft cooler.
> No new fuel, kit, shoes, or pacing in the final 4 weeks.

---

**Nutrition**

**Heat**

## D-0 — Saturday 19 Sep, race day

### Race-morning sequence

| Time before swim start | Action |
|---|---|
| ~3 hr | Wake. ~2–3 g CHO/kg of proven food (~160–240 g). Coffee if normal. |
| ~2.5 hr | Finish eating. Start hydration. |
| ~2 hr | Travel to race venue. |
| ~90 min | Bike check, final tyre pressure. Drop street wear at bag drop. |
| ~60 min | Toilet — go early, queues build. |
| ~45 min | Wetsuit on (if applicable). Last bodyglide. |
| ~30 min | 30–40 g CHO (Maurten or familiar). Ice slurry 300–500 ml. ~80 mg caffeine. |
| ~15 min | Into start corral. |
| Start | Calm. Easy first 200 m. |

### T1 protocol

- Helmet on FIRST.
- Shoes on bike (mounted) or in T1 bag — whichever rehearsed.
- Cold towel for neck before mounting.
- Ice slurry sip from T1 cooler.
- Race belt on. Watch on bike mode.

### Bike protocol

- First 90 min: cap IF ≤0.68. Average power ~200–210 W. Discipline.
- Bertinoro climbs: cap IF 0.78. Even if legs willing.
- Two bottles per aid station: one drink, one douse.
- Slow pour on head, neck, down front of tri-suit, on quads.
- Fuelling: 90–100 g CHO/hr. Watch alarm every 20 min.
- Sodium: 1,000–1,500 mg/hr in heat (provisional pending PH test).
- Caffeine: ~80 mg in final 30 min of bike.

### T2 protocol

- Helmet OFF, shoes ON.
- Cold towel for neck; ice-water dunk for cap/visor.
- Ice slurry sip from T2 cooler.
- Soft flask onto race belt.
- Caffeine ~80 mg from soft flask drink or chew.
- Move.

### Run protocol

- First 5 km ≥10 sec/km slower than goal pace. RPE/HR-led, not pace-led.
- Soft flask sip every 20 min on watch alarm. **No gels.**
- Water dousing every aid station from km 5 — head, neck, front + back of tri-suit, sponges in straps.
- Cola or course sports drink at every aid station from km 10. Continuous sips.
- Caffeine ~80 mg early run. Optional ~80 mg at km 21.
- 4 × 10 km loops — pre-plan exact intake at each station.

### Post-finish

- Walk 15 min. Get warm — body temp drops fast.
- Eat within 45 min — proven post-race food only.
- No running 10–14 days minimum.
