## Bike — 180 km, two loops via Bertinoro

### Course profile (GPS-confirmed from 2025 race)

| Section | Distance | Profile |
|---|---|---|
| Cervia → Salt Pans | 0–10 km | Flat coast. Pink flamingos. Likely tail/head wind dependent on the day. |
| Salt Pans → Forlimpopoli | 10–35 km | Flat-fast, slight rolling. |
| Forlimpopoli → Bertinoro | 35–55 km | **Sustained climb to Bertinoro.** Several km of rolling-up plus the final climb to ~50 m above Forlimpopoli. |
| Bertinoro top | 55 km | Brief plateau (~50 m through town). |
| Bertinoro → Cervia | 55–90 km | Descent + flat return. Fast section. |
| Lap 2 | 90–180 km | Repeat. |
| **Total elevation gain** | **1,082 m (GPS)** | Marketing says ~700 m, GPS says 1,082 m. Plan against the higher number. |

### Bertinoro climb data (from 2025 race-day power file)

Lap 1 climbs (when fresh):
- 98 sec @ 306 W (97% FTP) — short sharp pitch
- 180 sec @ 284 W (90% FTP) — longer climb section
- 195 sec @ 305 W (97% FTP) — final pitch into Bertinoro

Lap 2 climb attempt:
- 203 sec @ 240 W (76% FTP) — **already 60 W weaker than equivalent Lap 1 effort**

**Total joules above FTP across both laps (2025): 35,326 J.** Significant match-burning.

### Bike pacing rules (race-day 2026)

| Phase | Cap |
|---|---|
| First 90 min | **IF ≤0.68** (~215 W on FTP 316). 2025 cost of going harder: Lap 2 fade. |
| Bertinoro climbs | **IF ≤0.78** target (~246 W max). 2025 climbs at 285–305 W on Lap 1 = matches gone. **Hard rule: even if legs feel willing, cap.** |
| Lap 2 (post-90 min) | NP target ~225 W (IF 0.71). **VI target <1.05.** |
| Decoupling check | If decoupling >5% by km 120, drop NP by 5% for remainder. |

> **Gearing constraint (current 52T × 10–33):** the IF-0.78 cap is unworkable at gradients ≥6% with current gearing. At 80 rpm in lowest gear, 6% gradient demands ~280 W (88% FTP); 7% demands ~325 W (>100% FTP). **2025 race confirmed:** climb 3 was 56 rpm × 305 W — the gearing-floor signature. Either swap to a sub-compact chainring (46T or 48T) before June, OR accept above-cap power on steep pitches and budget the matches elsewhere (e.g. softer first 90 min, NP cap 220 W not 225 W on the rest of the ride). See `rules.md` and `risk-register.md` R14.

| Variable | Range | Plan |
|---|---|---|
