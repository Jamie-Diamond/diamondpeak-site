About this calculator

W/kg = FTP (watts) ÷ Body weight (kg)

FTP (Functional Threshold Power) represents the highest average power you can sustain for approximately one hour. The benchmark categories are based on widely-used cycling classification standards. Male and female ranges reflect physiological differences in typical power output at elite levels.
