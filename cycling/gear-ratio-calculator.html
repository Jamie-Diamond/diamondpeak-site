Formulae

Gear Ratio = Chainring teeth ÷ Sprocket teeth
Development = Gear Ratio × Wheel Circumference / 1000  (metres per pedal revolution)
Speed (km/h) = Gear Ratio × Circumference (mm) × Cadence (rpm) × 60 ÷ 1,000,000
Speed (mph) = Speed (km/h) ÷ 1.60934
