Training Stress Score
Calculator

Calculate your Training Stress Score (TSS) from normalised power, FTP, and ride duration. Use the reference tables to gauge recovery demand and plan weekly training load.

Methodology

IF = Normalised Power ÷ FTP
TSS = (Duration in seconds × NP × IF) ÷ (FTP × 3600) × 100

Training Stress Score was developed by Dr Andrew Coggan as a way to quantify the physiological cost of a workout relative to your current fitness (FTP). An IF of 1.0 means you rode at your threshold; a one-hour ride at exactly FTP yields a TSS of 100.
